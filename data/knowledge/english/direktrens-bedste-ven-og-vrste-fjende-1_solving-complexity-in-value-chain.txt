Topic: Solving Complexity in Value Chain
Source article: Direktørens bedste ven og værste fjende (1)
Keywords: value chain, complexity, analysis, profit, products

Collaborated with DTU Professor to analyze the value chain, resulting in halving product range to 50,000. Contrary to belief, increased EBITDA. Complexity arises as companies grow, losing track of profit sources. Unhealthy complexity stems from wrong customer-product mix. Uncontrolled complexity leads to inventory issues, low profits, high capital binding, and long lead times.
