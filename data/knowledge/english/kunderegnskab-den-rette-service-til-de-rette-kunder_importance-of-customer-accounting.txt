Topic: Importance of Customer Accounting
Source article: Kunderegnskab  Den rette service til de rette kunder
Keywords: customer analysis, profitability, customer retention, cost-to-serve, customer segmentation

Sales departments should analyze customers to avoid overservicing less profitable ones. Companies should identify key customers, enhance profitability through small changes, and start with a basic customer accounting that includes earnings, cost of goods sold, direct costs, and cost-to-serve.
