Topic: About Bygma Gruppen A/S
Source article: Bygma
Keywords: company overview, size, revenue, industry, Danish companies

Bygma Gruppen is among the Top 100 companies in Denmark, employing around 2,400 people across more than 100 business units in the Nordics. They are the largest Danish-owned supplier to the construction industry, with a turnover of approximately 7.9 billion DKK in 2018.
