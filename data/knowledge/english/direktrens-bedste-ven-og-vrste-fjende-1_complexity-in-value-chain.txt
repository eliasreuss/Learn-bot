Topic: Complexity in Value Chain
Source article: Direktørens bedste ven og værste fjende (1)
Keywords: complexity, value chain, growth, products, customers

1. Complexity in value chain arises as a company grows and adds more products to its portfolio.
2. Over time, companies lose track of which products are profitable due to the overwhelming number of products.
3. Complexity leads to a disconnection between customers and products, where low-margin items consume resources while high-margin ones are neglected.
