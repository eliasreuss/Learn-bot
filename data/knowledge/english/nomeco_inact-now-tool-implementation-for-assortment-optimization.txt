Topic: Inact Now Tool Implementation for Assortment Optimization
Source article: Nomeco
Keywords: Inact Now, assortment optimization, category management, ABC analysis

In 2014, Nomeco adopted Inact Now to ensure an active, dynamic, and attractive assortment of duty-free goods for pharmacies. This involved analyzing 40 categories, 7,000 product numbers, and collaborating with 350 suppliers.
