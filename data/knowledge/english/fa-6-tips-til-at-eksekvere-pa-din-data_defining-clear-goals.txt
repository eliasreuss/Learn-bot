Topic: Defining Clear Goals
Source article: Få 6 tips til at eksekvere på din data
Keywords: goals, mission, cause-and-effect diagram, project management

1. Define clear and understandable goals for your project to ensure satisfaction and alignment among team members. Start with a project mission such as improving revenue or reducing working capital, and break it down into milestones. Use cause-and-effect diagrams to translate the mission into tangible deliverables.
