Topic: Benefits of Standards
Source article: Er din virksomhed virkelig så unik – og skal den være det 
Keywords: standards, value creation, risk management, efficiency, SMV

Standards offer a shortcut to quick, measurable, and low-risk value creation as they are tried, tested, and developed based on experiences from numerous companies facing similar challenges. For small and medium-sized enterprises (SMV’er), implementing proven solutions allows for rapid progress without reinventing the wheel and taking unnecessary risks.
