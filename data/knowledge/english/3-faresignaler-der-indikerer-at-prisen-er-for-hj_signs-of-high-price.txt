Keywords: pricing, IT systems, consultants, cost indicators

#1: Difficulty understanding the work the provider will do for you can indicate a high price. Analytical IT systems or services may seem complex to inflate prices. Ensure basic understanding of the service.

#2: When the project expands beyond your original needs, costs can rise. Limiting scope to essential tasks leads to quicker results, shorter implementation time, and lower costs.

#3: Lack of transparency in cost breakdown by the supplier can be a tactic to increase prices. Ensure detailed insight into cost allocation.
