Topic: Data Analysis and Solutions Implementation
Source article: AUBO
Keywords: data analysis, solutions, inventory management, data insights, automation

Inact conducted a thorough analysis of AUBO's assortment and proposed solutions to address acute problems and long-term needs. They cleaned and updated the foundational data for correct procurement and disposition. AUBO's data and products were categorized for a simple overview, identifying inefficient processes and areas for improvement on the warehouse. Collaboration focused on gaining data insights and implementing automations to change behaviors. AUBO gained real-time inventory overview and ABC categorization of products, facilitating identification of dead stock, data-driven procurement optimization, and material reuse guidance.
