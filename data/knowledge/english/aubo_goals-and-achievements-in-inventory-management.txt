Topic: Goals and Achievements in Inventory Management
Source article: AUBO
Keywords: inventory reduction, dead stock management, ESG compliance, creative solutions, value addition

AUBO set goals to significantly reduce inventory by 20% and create a plan for dead stock management. The focus was on achieving these targets without compromising customer service. Results included a 20% reduction in inventory value, meeting management's expectations. The collaboration with Inact not only improved financials but also enhanced internal collaboration. AUBO's creative thinking was reinforced by data-driven decision-making.
