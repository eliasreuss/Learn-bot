**Topic: Insights**
**Source Article: Guide to Creating an Insight**
**Keywords: views, visualization, matrix-view, grid-view, kpi-view, sumsheet, profit-view, trend-view**

Content:
An Insight can visualize data in 6 different ways (views). Depending on the purpose, one or more of these views may be enabled or disabled by an administrator. The 6 views are:
1. Matrix-view
2. Grid-view
3. KPI-view
4. Sumsheet
5. Profit view
6. Trend View
