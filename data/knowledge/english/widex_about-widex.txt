Topic: About Widex
Source article: Widex
Keywords: company history, revenue, global presence, employee count, industry

Widex, founded in Denmark in 1956, is a leading global manufacturer of hearing aids. In 2017, the company had a revenue of 4346.2M DKK. Widex's hearing aids are sold in over 100 countries, with a global workforce of over 4,000 employees.
