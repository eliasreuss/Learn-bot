Topic: ESG and Responsible Production
Source article: AUBO
Keywords: ESG initiatives, responsible production, data-driven planning, waste reduction, competitive advantage

Inact and AUBO's collaboration led to a shift towards ESG and responsible production practices. Data-driven planning replaced emotional decision-making, aiding in inventory management and product lifecycle decisions. By reducing waste and optimizing inventory, AUBO progressed towards a more responsible production approach, including packaging. This strategic shift was seen as a crucial aspect of AUBO's future strategy and a potential competitive edge in the industry.
