Topic: Symptoms of Supply Chain Variability at Alfa Laval
Source article: Alfa-Laval
Keywords: supply chain, symptoms, inventory management, data insight, Excel

Alfa Laval experienced symptoms of supply chain variability, including high inventory levels, inaccuracies, long customer lead times, lack of data insight, reliance on Excel, and customer satisfaction as the primary focus. Decisions were often based on incomplete or unstable data, leading to suboptimal outcomes.
