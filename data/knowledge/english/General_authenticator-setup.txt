Content:
Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your Inact Now account.
How to enable it:
1. Install an authenticator app like "Microsoft Authenticator" from your phone's app store.
2. In Inact Now, go to your settings and tap "Enable 2FA".
3. Open the Microsoft Authenticator app on your phone.
4. Tap on "+" or "Add account" and select the account type "work/school".
5. Tap on "Scan QR code" and scan the QR code displayed on the screen in Inact Now.
6. The app will now show a 6-digit code that changes every 30 seconds. Enter this code in Inact Now to confirm the setup.
