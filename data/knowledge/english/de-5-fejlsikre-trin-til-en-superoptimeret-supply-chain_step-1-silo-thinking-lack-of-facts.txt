Topic: Step 1: Silo Thinking & Lack of Facts
Source article: De 5 fejlsikre trin til en superoptimeret supply chain
Keywords: silo thinking, lack of data, decision-making, organizational alignment

Companies at this stage focus on topline growth and supply security, operating with unnecessarily high inventory levels and low turnover. Decisions are often based on gut feelings rather than data. Silo thinking is prevalent, with sales, procurement, and logistics operating independently.
