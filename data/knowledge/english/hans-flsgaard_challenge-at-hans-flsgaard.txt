Topic: Challenge at Hans Følsgaard
Source article: Hans Følsgaard
Keywords: Hans Følsgaard, Net Working Capital, inventory management, capital binding, AddTech

Hans Følsgaard aimed to improve capital binding by reducing a heavy inventory of 118 million DKK. Focus on optimizing creditors and debtors had already shown results. As a member of AddTech, the company needed to be accountable for every NWC-bound krone.
