Topic: Policies Development
Source article: Direktørens bedste ven og værste fjende (1)
Keywords: policies development, resource allocation, growth strategies, strategic decision-making, IT systems parameters

Develop policies to prioritize the right customers and products for effective resource allocation, growth, and profitability. Policies provide clear guidelines, expectations for employees, ensuring consistent and strategic decision-making across company functions. Policies may impact parameters in IT systems like discount schemes, inventory management, etc.
