Topic: Impact on Sustainability and Responsible Production
Source article: AUBO
Keywords: ESG, sustainable production, data-driven decisions, waste reduction, competitive advantage

AUBO's work with Inact and the Inact Now platform nudged the company towards ESG and responsible production practices. Data-driven plans were made for products based on sustainability criteria. By reducing waste and optimizing inventory management, AUBO progressed towards more responsible production, including packaging. This strategic shift is seen as a crucial competitive factor in the industry.
