Topic: Unique Aspects of Companies
Source article: Er din virksomhed virkelig så unik – og skal den være det 
Keywords: unique, companies, standard solutions, complexity, value creation

Companies often believe they are unique due to nuances like special customer relationships, complex supply chains, technical products, or specialized employees. While each company has its uniqueness, certain elements are common across most companies. Implementing fundamental principles and processes based on best practices can address up to 80% of structural challenges, with the remaining 20% focusing on adapting to the company's unique DNA.
