Topic: Step 2: Product Profitability & Supplier Collaboration
Source article: De 5 fejlsikre trin til en superoptimeret supply chain
Keywords: product profitability, ABC analysis, strategic decisions, supplier relationships

Transition from stage 1 to 2 occurs when a company realizes the importance of profitable growth and differentiated market servicing. Focus shifts to analyzing product profitability, identifying key products, and establishing strategic decision-making frameworks. Supplier collaboration is systematized based on product importance.
