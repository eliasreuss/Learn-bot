Topic: Hvalkurve Tool
Source article: Direktørens bedste ven og værste fjende (1)
Keywords: Hvalkurve, profitability, product, customer, visualization

1. The Hvalkurve tool visually represents a company's profit and loss based on product and customer composition.
2. It helps identify which products and customers contribute to profitability.
3. The tool assists in understanding where a company is making or losing money.
