Topic: Heat Map for Strategic Planning
Source article: Direktørens bedste ven og værste fjende (1)
Keywords: heat map, strategy, visualization, profitability, resource allocation

1. A heat map provides an overview of a company's earnings structure. 
2. Helps identify growth areas by focusing on lucrative customer segments or developing profitable products. 
3. Assists in resource allocation decisions by highlighting top contributing customers and products.
