Topic: Results and Benefits of Collaboration
Source article: AUBO
Keywords: inventory value reduction, collaboration improvement, automated reporting, data-driven decisions, creative solutions

AUBO reduced inventory value by 20% in a year without affecting production or customer service. The collaboration with Inact not only improved financial results but also enhanced internal cooperation. Automated reporting streamlined communication between departments, allowing focus on inventory management. Data-driven decisions supported creativity, strengthening AUBO's ability to innovate with tangible data. The collaboration also paved the way for better ESG and responsible production practices.
