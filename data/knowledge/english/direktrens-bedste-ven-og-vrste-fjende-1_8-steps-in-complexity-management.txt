Topic: 8 steps in Complexity Management
Source article: Direktørens bedste ven og værste fjende (1)
Keywords: complexity management, steps, guidelines, business strategy

1. Define key KPIs and criteria for evaluating customers and products. 2. Segment and prioritize customer base and product portfolio. 3. Understand relationships between customers and products. 4. Develop policies for prioritizing customers and products. 5. Allocate resources based on prioritized segments. 6. Foster interdisciplinary collaboration. 7. Provide training for employees. 8. Align resource usage with overall strategy.
