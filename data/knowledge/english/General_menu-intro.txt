Content:
You can access 5 main menu items in Inact Now:
1. Home: Provides a quick overview of your key KPIs.
2. Insights: Displays all the reports (Insights) you have access to.
3. Actions: Shows a "to-do" list with topics you should focus on.
4. Dashboard: Displays all company Dashboards.
5. Download: Here you can download data to your PC.
Some users by default only have access to 'Home'. To access all menu items, ask your administrator to enable it.
