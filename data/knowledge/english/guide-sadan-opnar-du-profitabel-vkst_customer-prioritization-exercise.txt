Topic: Customer Prioritization Exercise
Source article: Guide  Sådan opnår du profitabel vækst
Keywords: customer prioritization, alignment, improvement, transparency, consensus

1. Select five random customers. 2. Prioritize the customers from 1-5, where 1 is the most important. 3. Compare how colleagues in procurement, sales, or production would prioritize the same customers. 4. Misalignment indicates improvement potential in the company.
