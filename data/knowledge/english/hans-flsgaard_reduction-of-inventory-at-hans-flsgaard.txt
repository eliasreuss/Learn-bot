Topic: Reduction of Inventory at Hans Følsgaard
Source article: Hans Følsgaard
Keywords: inventory reduction, supply chain management, financial improvement, decision-making, company culture

Hans Følsgaard reduced inventory from 118 million to 88 million DKK by the end of the 2023-2024 fiscal year. This led to better collaboration in procurement and sales, improved supply chain management, potential for further bottom-line improvement, and a shift towards more profitable and fact-based decision-making.
