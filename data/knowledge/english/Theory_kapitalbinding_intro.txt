**Topic: Supply Chain Management**
  
**Source Article: Guide to Capital Binding**  

**Keywords:** supply chain, balance, service, costs, profit, customer satisfaction, growth, inventory  

Content:  
As a supply chain manager, the most important task is to ensure the perfect balance between service, costs, and profit. These three factors together play a crucial role in customer satisfaction and the company's profitability. The ability to balance supply and demand in a profitable way is essential for competitiveness. There is often a conflict between the sales department, which wants high inventories to avoid long lead times, and the finance department, which wants low inventories to avoid high costs and write-offs.
