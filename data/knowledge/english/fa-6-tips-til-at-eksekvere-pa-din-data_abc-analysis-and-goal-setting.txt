Topic: ABC Analysis and Goal Setting
Source article: Få 6 tips til at eksekvere på din data
Keywords: ABC analysis, goal setting, prioritization, service level, inventory management

Run your ABC analysis and set goals for each ABC category to support the overall mission. 1. Define the overall goal for your service level. 2. Set a higher service level goal for AA, BA, and CA products. 3. Set a relatively lower service level goal for the rest to maintain an average of 95%.
