Topic: Unhealthy Complexity
Source article: Direktørens bedste ven og værste fjende (1)
Keywords: unhealthy complexity, growth, profitability, LEGO case, product portfolio

1. Unhealthy complexity harms a company and arises during growth spurts.
2. LEGO's case exemplifies tackling unhealthy complexity by streamlining the product portfolio and focusing on core activities.
3. Activity-based costing helped LEGO remove non-value-adding products, leading to significant financial turnaround.
