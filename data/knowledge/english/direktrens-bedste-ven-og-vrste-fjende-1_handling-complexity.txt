Topic: Handling Complexity
Source article: Direktørens bedste ven og værste fjende (1)
Keywords: complexity management, symptoms, causes, strategies, organization

To address complexity, start by understanding symptoms like low profitability, long lead times, and unsatisfied customers. Causes include extensive product/customer mix, lack of insight, and a 'more is more' mindset. Strategies involve analyzing where the company is losing money and optimizing resources effectively.
