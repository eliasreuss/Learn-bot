Topic: Understanding Heat Maps
Source article: Direktørens bedste ven og værste fjende (1)
Keywords: heat map, visualization, profitability, insight, strategy

1. Heat maps visualize a company's profitability based on selected dimensions like product and customer groups. 
2. Use color codes (green, yellow, red) for profit, neutral, and loss areas. 
3. Provides insights for optimizing business strategies and identifying areas for improvement.
