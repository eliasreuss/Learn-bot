Topic: Importance of Competence in Supply Chain Planning
Source article: Alfa-Laval
Keywords: competence, supply chain planning, data utilization, core competency, Digital Supply Chain Office

Supply chain planning is not a core competency for Alfa Laval, emphasizing the need for expertise in managing data and processes effectively. The establishment of a Digital Supply Chain Office in 2021 aimed to enhance digitalization efforts and support factories in adopting supply chain best practices.
