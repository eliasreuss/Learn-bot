Topic: Customer and Product Relationships
Source article: Direktørens bedste ven og værste fjende (1)
Keywords: customer relationships, product relationships, revenue impact, customer segmentation

A-customers: Few in number, high revenue and profit. Purchase products mainly from A-segment, with some from C-segment. Contribute significantly to total revenue. C-customers: Majority in number, low revenue and profit. Buy products across A, B, and C segments, contributing minimally to total revenue.
