Topic: Avoiding Silo Optimization
Source article: Er din virksomhed virkelig så unik – og skal den være det 
Keywords: silo optimization, best practice, organization, value chain, leadership

Optimizing standards in isolation without connecting them to the entire value chain can lead to shifting problems instead of solving them. Best practices should be integrated horizontally across the organization, serving as a common language and direction spanning departments. It encompasses technology, frameworks, processes, language, behavior, culture, competencies, and starts with leadership.
